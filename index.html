Kielbasa N more

Aleksandra Paulina & Daniel

Aleksandra Paulina and Daniel love to cook and feed people passionately. These two foodies are cousins always discussing smoker wood and discovering a shared commitment to all things delicious. This passion has led them to do pop-up dinners, private parties, and catering events. And now the Kielbasa' n More food truck is rolling down your way.

The Magic Truck

We are not a restaurant, but we serve fantastic Polish food cooked with magic and filled with love, something you wouldn't normally expect from a food truck. We absolutely enjoy creating variations of the best Polish dishes based on products of the highest quality from local bakeries and butchers. We eat what we cook daily and it makes us happy.

Quality Ingredients

Great-tasting Polish food is based on three major elements, quality ingredients, proven recipes, and a big heart for cooking.
